Preloaders

CSS and SVG based material design style preloaders.

You can check the live preview at /preloaders.html

Basic example

Preloaders are available in multiple color variants.

<!-- Light -->
<div class="preloader preloader--light">
    <svg viewBox="25 25 50 50">
        <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="20" />
    </svg>
</div>

Sizing

Preloaders are also available in 4 custom sizes.

<!-- Small -->
<div class="preloader preloader--sm preloader--light">
    <svg viewBox="25 25 50 50">
        <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="20" />
    </svg>
</div>

<!-- Default -->
<div class="preloader preloader--light">
    <svg viewBox="25 25 50 50">
        <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="20" />
    </svg>
</div>
