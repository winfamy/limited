Progress Bar

Provide up-to-date feedback on the progress of a workflow or action with simple yet flexible progress bars.

Progress Bar is a Bootstrap component and you can find it's complete documentation with more examples at: http://getbootstrap.com/javascript/#progress

Basic example

<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 60%;">
      <span class="sr-only">60% Complete</span>
    </div>
</div>

Contextual alternatives

<!-- Success -->
<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-success" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="40" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 40%"></div>
</div>

<!-- Info -->
<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-info" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="20" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 20%"></div>
</div>

<!-- Warning -->
<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-warning" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 60%"></div>
</div>

<!-- Danger -->
<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-danger" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="80" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 80%"></div>
</div>

Striped Progress bars

Uses a gradient to create a striped effect with an additional modifier class .progress-bar-striped. Gradient striped is not available in IE9 and below.

<div class="progress">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-success progress-bar-striped" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="40" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 40%">
    <span class="sr-only">40% Complete (success)</span>
</div>

Stacked Progress bar

Place multiple bars into the same .progress to stack them.

<div class="progress">
  <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-success" style="width: 35%">
    <span class="sr-only">35% Complete (success)</span>
  </div>
  <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-warning progress-bar-striped" style="width: 20%">
    <span class="sr-only">20% Complete (warning)</span>
  </div>
  <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-danger" style="width: 10%">
    <span class="sr-only">10% Complete (danger)</span>
  </div>
</div>
